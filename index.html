Yasemin Varlik is born in Turkey, raised in Geneva, Switzerland. She has a BFA in Communication Design and a minoring in BFA Photography from Parsons School of Design, NY. She has worked in various design fields, varying from an advertising agency, TBWA United State of Fans based in Amsterdam and an editorial/graphic design intern at Mission Magazine, New York.

Awards and Involvement

She has been awarded with the Type Directors Club Scholarship in 2018. She is in the executive board of the school chapter for NSLS(National Society of Leadership and Success), which is a large established leadership honor society known globally.
