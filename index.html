Yasemin Varlik is born in Turkey, raised in Geneva, Switzerland. She has a BFA in Communication Design and a minor in BFA Photography from Parsons School of Design, NY. She is currently enrolled in MA Graphic Communications at Central Saint Martins, UAL. She has worked in various design fields, varying from an advertising agency, TBWA United State of Fans based in Amsterdam and an art editor/graphic design intern at Mission Magazine based in New York.
