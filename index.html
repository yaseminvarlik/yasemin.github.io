Yasemin Varlik is a graphic designer currently based in London, UK. She was born in Turkey, raised in Geneva, Switzerland.

She has a BFA in Communication Design and a minor in BFA Photography from Parsons School of Design, NY. She is currently enrolled in MA Graphic Communications at Central Saint Martins, UAL.
–

Her work focuses on designing visual identities, branding, printed matter, publications, small animations, hand lettering, social media content and websites.
