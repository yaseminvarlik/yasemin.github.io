Yasemin Varlik is born in Turkey, raised in Geneva, Switzerland. She has a BFA in Communication Design and a minor in BFA Photography from Parsons School of Design, NY. She is currently enrolled in MA Graphic Communications at Central Saint Martins, UAL.
—
She has worked in various design fields; Graphic Design Intern at Vault49 and TBWA United State of Fans and as an Art Editor/ Designer at Mission Magazine based in New York. She developed a brand identity using custom type for Studio Archives, an online vintage clothing sales brand.

Currently doing illustration and design work for a publishing company; Raskol'un Baltasi, as well as working on Unknown Quantities 8, which is an annual collaborative publication done by MACCC and MAGCD students at UAL. She is a type freak, who loves to explore the tension between from and perception.
