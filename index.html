Yasemin Varlik is born in Turkey, raised in Geneva, Switzerland. She has a BFA in Communication Design and a minor in BFA Photography from Parsons School of Design, NY. She is currently enrolled in MA Graphic Communications at Central Saint Martins, UAL. She has worked in various design fields;
Graphic design intern at TBWA United State of Fans and Vault49 and an art editor/graphic design intern at Mission Magazine based in New York.

Currently doing illustration work for a publishing company; Raskolun Baltasi and custom type and graphics for Studio Archives, an online vintage clothing sales brand.
