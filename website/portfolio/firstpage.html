YASEMIN VARLIK

Yasemin Varlik is born in Turkey, raised in Geneva, Switzerland. Since the age of three, her aim was always to become an artist. After long dedication and effort she has put in to her education, she is now studying Communication Design at Parsons School of Design in New York. Even though, she experienced different art principles,she always leaned towards in the direction of graphic design to pursue her dreams.
